## 1. Introduction

LifeBoat ("we," "our," "us," or the "Service") is a Chrome browser extension for digital account management and estate planning. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

**By installing or using LifeBoat, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.** If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not install or use the Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored locally in your browser and encrypted with your master password:

- Account credentials and login information you choose to save
- Vault contents (passwords, 2FA codes, secure notes)
- Trusted contact information (names, email addresses)
- User preferences and settings

**This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (Pro feature).**

### 2.2 Information Transmitted to Our Servers

For registered users, we collect:

- **Email address:** For account authentication and communication
- **Encrypted vault backups:** If you enable cloud sync (Pro users only). These are encrypted on your device before transmission; we cannot decrypt this data.
- **Subscription and billing status:** Managed through Stripe; we do not store payment card details

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Browsing history or browsing activity
- Cookies or tracking identifiers
- Unencrypted passwords or credentials
- Government identification documents
- Biometric data
- Location data

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage statistics such as:

- Feature usage patterns (not tied to individual users)
- Error reports and crash logs
- General geographic region (country-level only)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
- Process transactions and manage subscriptions
- Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
- Send notifications to your designated trusted contacts when triggered
- Respond to customer support inquiries
- Comply with legal obligations

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.**

---

## 4. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

LifeBoat uses a zero-knowledge encryption architecture:

- Your master password is never transmitted to or stored on our servers
- All sensitive data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption with PBKDF2 key derivation (310,000 iterations)
- We cannot access, read, or decrypt your vault contents
- If you lose your master password, we cannot recover your data
