# LifeBoat Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## What We Collect

- **Account domains** - We detect which websites you log into (e.g., "google.com", "facebook.com"). We never collect passwords.
- **Contact information** - Your name, email, and phone number (stored locally on your device)
- **Heir information** - Names and contact details of people you designate as heirs (stored locally)

## What We DON'T Collect

- ❌ Passwords or login credentials
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Personal messages or content
- ❌ Financial information

## Data Storage

- **Free Plan**: All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- **Pro Plan**: Optional encrypted cloud backup (you control when to sync)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your heir information is only shared with your designated heirs when our inactivity detection system is triggered according to your settings.

## Third-Party Services

- **Stripe** - Payment processing (Pro subscriptions only)
- **SendGrid** - Email notifications
- **Twilio** - SMS notifications (Pro only)

## Your Rights

You can:
- Export all your data anytime
- Delete all your data with one click
- Disable the extension at any time
